Here's your itinerary

Varanasi-The City of Devotion

Day 1: Arrival and Ganges River Evening Ceremony

  • Morning: Arrive in Varanasi and check into your accommodation.
  • Afternoon: Explore the bustling streets of Varanasi, visit the local markets, and try some street food.
  • Evening: Witness the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti ceremony on the ghats of the Ganges River. Participate in a boat ride to see the ceremony from the river, offering a unique perspective.

Day 2: Spiritual Exploration

  • Morning: Visit the Kashi Vishwanath Temple, one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Afternoon: Explore Sarnath, where Buddha gave his first sermon. Visit the Dhamek Stupa, Mulagandha Kuti Vihar, and Sarnath Archaeological Museum.
  • Evening: Return to Varanasi and spend some time exploring the city's vibrant street life and markets.

Day 3: Cultural Immersion

  • Morning: Take a sunrise boat ride on the Ganges River to witness the ghats come to life with morning rituals and activities.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Banaras Hindu University (BHU) campus and the Bharat Kala Bhavan Museum to learn about the art and culture of Varanasi.
  • Evening: Attend a classical music or dance performance to experience the rich cultural heritage of the city.

Day 4: Temples and Silk

  • Morning: Explore the historic temples of Varanasi, including the Durga Temple, Sankat Mochan Hanuman Temple, and Tulsi Manas Temple.
  • Afternoon: Visit the Weaver's Village to see the intricate process of weaving Banarasi silk sarees. You can also shop for souvenirs and traditional textiles.
  • Evening: Enjoy a leisurely stroll along the ghats, taking in the sights and sounds of the city.

Day 5: Varanasi Exploration

  • Morning: Visit the Ramnagar Fort, located on the eastern bank of the Ganges River. Explore the fort and its museum, which houses a collection of vintage cars, royal artifacts, and manuscripts.
  • Afternoon: Take a guided walking tour of Varanasi's narrow lanes, visiting hidden temples, traditional workshops, and old havelis (mansions).
  • Evening: Enjoy a farewell dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the Ganges River, reflecting on your memorable experiences in Varanasi.
