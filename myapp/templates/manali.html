Here's your itinerary

Manali-Where Every Season Paints a Different Dream

Day 1: Arrival and Local Exploration

  • Morning: Arrive in Manali and check into your accommodation.
  • Afternoon: Explore the local markets like Mall Road and Old Manali. Visit Hadimba Temple, a beautiful wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  • Evening: Enjoy dinner at a local restaurant and stroll around the town.

Day 2: Solang Valley Adventure

  • Morning: Head to Solang Valley for adventure activities like paragliding, zorbing, and horse riding.
  • Afternoon: Have lunch at one of the cafes in Solang Valley and continue with more activities or relax amidst the stunning views.
  • Evening:Return to Manali and spend a relaxed evening exploring more of the town's charm..

Day 3: Rohtang Pass Excursion

  • Morning: Depart early for a day trip to Rohtang Pass, enjoying breathtaking landscapes along the way.
  • Afternoon: Spend time at Rohtang Pass, indulge in snow activities (if visiting in the appropriate season), and soak in the panoramic views.
  • Evening: Return to Manali and unwind with a warm meal and perhaps a leisurely walk.

Day 4: Naggar Castle and Art Exploration/h5>

  • Morning: Visit Naggar Castle, a historic royal residence with stunning architecture and panoramic views of the Kullu Valley.
  • Afternoon: Explore Naggar's art galleries, showcasing traditional and contemporary Himalayan art.
  • Evening: Return to Manali and relax with dinner at a local eatery.

Day 5: Manikaran Excursion

  • Morning:Take a day trip to Manikaran, known for its hot springs and spiritual significance for both Sikhs and Hindus..
  • Afternoon: Visit the Gurudwara and temples in Manikaran and enjoy a dip in the hot springs.
  • Evening: Return to Manali and spend your last evening exploring any spots you may have missed or simply unwind.
